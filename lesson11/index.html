Idaho... Where Adventures Come to Life

You probably hear "Idaho" and think "potatoes," and not much else. But in truth, Idaho is a magical place where adventures are not fantasy, they are reality. The stories of Idahoans are shared thought the earth as though they were fantasy adventures told in a distant past or future, or a different land altogether where magic is real and thrives. But these stories told as though fiction are not fiction or fantasy at all, they are true and occur in a land and time much closer here and now then you would ever image.

Travel to Idaho, and you will find mystical places where adventures are played out every day. Not only are the characters you know and love real, but you can meet them, find them on the streets of Idaho's cities. You can find the greatest of all characters in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. It is the world's greatest secret, and I am sharing it with you now. Be careful who you tell, for there are many that would like to mistreat the magical lands of adventure.

Written By Jayne Dough
