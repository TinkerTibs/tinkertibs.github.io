Website Planning Assignment

Target Audience Description

The target audience of the summertimetowintertide.com website:

Who: Parents, couples, towns members, and visitors interesting in learning what upcoming events are available to attend in the cities. Hikers, bikers, skiers, snowboarders, towns members and visitors interesting in knowing the current and forecasted weather within the given cities.

Age: 12 to 100

Technology: Our audience primarily uses their cellular devices to access information. The second most common is tablets and computers.

Income: Income levels will vary from the young unemployed, to the $60,000 household and above.

Motivation: To plan their upcoming activates, and ensure they are dressed properly for the occasion. To determine weather conditions for outdoor activities.

Personas 1

Jon Dough

Occupation: High school student

Demographics: Age: 16. Is currently working towards graduating high school. Middle sibling in his family's home, and has a stead girlfriend of one and half years.

Goals: Jon wants to spend time with his girlfriend and be ready to hit the slopes as soon as possible. He uses the website to learn about new events in the area that he can take his girlfriend to, and to keep track of the weather in winter so that he knows when there will be enough snow outside to go skiing.

Environment: Jon is very active and loves being outdoors. He hikes regularly in the summer, skis in the winter, and plays ball in his free time. Jon mostly uses his phone when he needs to look things up online because it's always with him.

Quote: "I want to find new things to do with my girlfriend away from our families, and be notified when there is enough snow on the ground to ski."

Personas 2

Jayne Dough

Occupation: Homemaker

Demographics: Age: 40. Has an associate's degree in general education. Married with three children.

Goals: Jayne wants to spend time with her family outside the home. She uses the website to learn about new events in the area that her family can attend together.

Environment: Jayne is very active and loves being outdoors. She's involved with the PTA at the schools her kids attend, and serves faithfully in her church. Jayne mostly uses her phone when she needs to look things up online because it is usually conveniently located near her person, though, occasionally she will use the family computer.

Quote: "I want to find fun things to do that everyone in my family will enjoy."

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Is there anything new I can do this weekend to get out of the house?

Scenario 2: What's the weather going to be like when I go dirt biking this weekend?

Scenario 3: What can we do while we are on vacation in Soda Springs?

Scenario 4: What's the weather going to be like when I go snowboarding this weekend?

Scenario 5: I missed the concert in the park last week, how did the event go?
