Website Planning Assignment

Site Name

Summertime to Wintertide
In all weather, we'll be your guide!

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the summertimetowintertide.com website will be to convey a current weather story, related to the area where the towns preside, and general information on the three towns. This objective will be fulfilled on the home page by the inclusion of a weather article relevant to the cities, and an information section for each town of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven which all reside in Idaho.

A second purpose is to provide town specific weather conditions and other town information for each town listed. This objective will be fulfilled through town specific pages that include a forecast of the anticipated weather, articles on recent events that have occurred in the town, and a list of upcoming events within the town.

The summertimetowintertide.com website will be used to inform readers about current weather conditions, and events about each town of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

Domain Name

The site name will be summertimetowintertide.com a domain which is available for $12.00 per year.
