Design Principles

PARC: Proximity

Heat Transfer Warehouse
heattransferwarehouse.com

The principle of proximity is illustrated on this site by its spacing between sections which clearly separates the searchable 'categories' from their 'popular brands', and so on throughout their home page.

PARC: Alignment

Heat Transfer Warehouse
heattransferwarehouse.com

The principle of alignment is represented on this site by its noticeable horizonal lines created by its photos and labels, and the vertical edges which remain consistent in the header, body, and footer.

PARC: Repetition

Heat Transfer Warehouse
heattransferwarehouse.com

The principle of repetition is exemplified on this site by its repeated implementation of circular and square photographs for different sections, constant heading conditions, and the use of a constant font style.
